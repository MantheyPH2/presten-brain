**Deliverable:** `02 - Tiger Tournaments/Projects/Rankings/Event Strength Phase 2 — Scope and Plan.md`

**Phase 2 primary change:** K-factor scaling per game based on event strength percentile.

```
K_adjusted = K_base × (0.70 + (strength_percentile / 100) × 0.60)
```

This gives:
- p50 events: K_adjusted = K_base (neutral, no change)
- p90 High-strength events: K_adjusted = 1.24 × K_base
- p10 Low-strength events: K_adjusted = 0.76 × K_base

Maximum per-game Phase 2 adjustment: ±30% of K_base (U13: max ±7.2 pts above baseline per game). Well within calibration noise — the GA ASPIRE distortion case was ~40 pts systematic across all games, not a proportional per-game adjustment.

Scaling applies symmetrically to wins and losses (correct: high-strength events both reward wins more and penalize losses less).
