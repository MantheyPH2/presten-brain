## Tasks Completed

### 1. Rank Bands Design
**Deliverable:** `02 - Tiger Tournaments/Projects/Rankings/Rank Bands Design.md`

**Key decisions:**
- **Approach B (rating-value bands)** selected over USA Rank's rank-position bands. Rating-based thresholds are consistent across age groups — Gold means the same quality in U13G and U17B. USA Rank's approach makes Gold depend on how many teams exist in each age group.
- **Thresholds:** Gold ≥1500, Silver 1350–1499, Bronze 1200–1349, Red 1050–1199, Blue 900–1049, Green <900. Grounded in Glicko v7 calibration values (MLS NEXT top at +160, Pre-ECNL at +25) and expected distribution from baseline ~1000.
- **Colors confirmed:** Gold `#FFD700`, Silver `#C0C0C0`, Bronze `#CD7F32`, Red `#DC2626`, Blue `#2563EB`, Green `#16A34A`
- **Storage:** SQL view `rankings_with_bands` — not a stored column. Avoids migration cost during threshold tuning phase.
- **Within-band rank:** Yes for Gold/Silver/Bronze (e.g., "Silver #37"). Red/Blue/Green do not display within-band rank.
- **Inactivity:** 7 months (matches USA Rank). Inactive teams hide their band badge.
- **Implementation estimate:** ~5.5 hours for Presten. Achievable before DSS (May 16).

**Action required before implementation:** Presten runs the distribution query to validate thresholds against actual data. Thresholds may shift slightly if median differs from ~1050.

### 2. Club Rankings Design
**Deliverable:** `02 - Tiger Tournaments/Projects/Rankings/Club Rankings Design.md`

**Key decisions:**
- **Club identity:** Hybrid approach — GotSport org_id as primary key (covers ~70–80% of teams), normalized name matching as fallback. New `clubs` + `team_club_assignments` tables.
- **Separate boys/girls rankings.** Combined ranking would disadvantage gender-specific clubs (LAFC boys-only, many ECNL clubs girls-only).
- **Methodology matches USA Rank baseline:** Average of top team ratings, U11–U17, minimum 5 of 7 age groups. Top team per age group = single highest-rated team (not average of all teams in that age group).
- **Activity requirement:** Team must have played in last 7 months to count — prevents stale elite teams from propping up a club's score.
- **Full SQL CTE chain** provided — 6-step computation ready to run after `compute-rankings.js`.
- **Bootstrap plan:** Week 1 seed from GotSport org_id, Week 2 name-based fallback, ongoing manual verification for top clubs.
- **Implementation estimate:** ~22 hours across 4 weeks. Launch by May 23 (one week after DSS).

**Watch out:** Coverage depends on whether GotSport org_id is already stored in the `teams` table. Presten must run `SELECT club, gotsport_org_id FROM teams LIMIT 20` to confirm column presence before starting.

### 3. Event Strength Ratings Design
**Deliverable:** `02 - Tiger Tournaments/Projects/Rankings/Event Strength Ratings Design.md`

**Key decisions:**
- **4 required metrics fully defined:**
  - Average Team Rating (numeric + percentile)
  - Strength Classification: High (≥p80) / Average (p40–p79) / Low (<p40) — within age group × gender
  - Competitive Spread: Tight (<75 StdDev) / Mixed (75–150) / Wide (>150)
  - Upset Rate: Low (<20%) / Average (20–40%) / High (>40%), with 50-point minimum rating gap to filter noise
- **No events table assumption:** Design works whether `events` is a standalone table with a PK or just `games.event_name`. Fallback query provided.
- **Historical ratings:** No `ratings_history` table exists. Current ratings used as approximation. Labeled transparently in UI. Phase 2 item: add nightly rating snapshots for future historical accuracy.
- **Ranking engine integration: Display-only for Phase 1.** SOS (Phase 7) already captures the core signal. Feedback loop creates circular dependency and would require full Elo recompute. Phase 2 (6+ months out): evaluate K-factor modifier using `strength_percentile`.
- **Edge cases all handled:** Small events, all-draw events, unranked teams, multi-age-group events, re-runs.
- **Rollout:** Current season first (2025-2026), then backfill. Nightly job after rankings run.
- **Implementation estimate:** ~13 hours. Presten can demo event strength cards at DSS if started April 28.

**We go further than USA Rank:** They show labels. We show the math (percentile, spread StdDev, upset rate as %). This is a competitive advantage — transparent methodology is one of our Phase 2 weapons.

All three systems use 7 months as the inactivity threshold, matching USA Rank. This ensures consistency — a team inactive enough to lose its band is also excluded from club rankings and event strength calculations.
