## What's Working on Fintwit Right Now

### Content Formats That Go Viral
1. **"Here's what nobody is talking about" threads** — contrarian framing + data = bookmarks + shares
2. **Annotated chart screenshots** — visual, scannable, shareable
3. **Earnings preview/recap threads** — people search for these in real time
4. **"I lost $X and here's what I learned" posts** — vulnerability + lesson = trust
5. **Data visualizations** — on-chain data, options flow, sector heatmaps
6. **Controversial rankings** — "Top 5 cryptos for 2026" always gets engagement
7. **Real-time trade journaling** — post entry, post exit, post P&L. Transparency wins.

### What NOT to Do (studied from failing accounts)
- Don't shill random tokens — instant credibility death
- Don't only post wins — people smell BS
- Don't use robot emoji spam (🚀🔥💰) — screams scam
- Don't post at wrong times (our TSLA mistake)
- Don't copy-paste the same format daily — gets stale
- Don't engage with bot/scam accounts — guilt by association

### Monetization Patterns From Successful Accounts

**The Pentoshi Model (533K followers):**
- Free public calls build credibility → paid Discord ($100-200/mo)
- Never does paid promos — trust IS the product
- Revenue estimated $500K-1M/yr from community alone

**The 10K Diver Model (300K followers):**
- Long educational threads → newsletter → course
- No trade calls, pure education. Different moat.
- Revenue from Substack + course sales

**The Options Hawk Model:**
- Real-time options flow → paid service ($99-299/mo)
- Data-backed, not opinion-backed
- Revenue from subscriptions + institutional clients

**The MMCrypto Model (1.7M followers):**
- X + YouTube cross-promotion
- Sponsored content (carefully selected)
- Massive reach but lower trust than Pentoshi
