## TWEET 5: Educational — Options (Single Tweet)

### Tweet: How to Use a Bull Call Spread
Options 101: Why I use bull call spreads instead of straight calls.

Example: $AMZN Apr 30 $260/$275 spread
• Buy $260 call: costs $9
• Sell $275 call: receive $3
• Net cost: $6 (vs $9)
• Max profit: $9 (if AMZN hits $275)

You cap your upside — but you cut your cost 33%.

For directional plays: spreads > naked calls. Always.
