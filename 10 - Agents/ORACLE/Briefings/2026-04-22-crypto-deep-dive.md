## Macro Context

### The Fed Factor — Warsh Nomination
Kevin Warsh, a hawkish former Fed Governor and prominent critic of quantitative easing, is the frontrunner to replace Jerome Powell as Federal Reserve Chair. Markets interpret a Warsh Fed as:
- Higher rates for longer
- Reduced accommodation for risk assets
- Dollar strength → Bitcoin weakness

This is the primary macro overhang on crypto. If Warsh is not confirmed, or if he signals moderation, crypto would reprice sharply higher. The uncertainty itself is the problem — markets price in the worst case.

### Geopolitical Risk — Iran-US
Renewed Iran-US tensions are creating a geopolitical risk premium that pushes institutional investors toward cash and away from risk assets. Bitcoin has historically been both a risk-on AND safe-haven asset at different times — right now it is being treated as risk-on, meaning it suffers in a geopolitical stress event.
