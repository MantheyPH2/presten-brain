# Cash Account Strategy — The Path to $25K

**We start as a cash account. No margin. No spreads. No shortcuts. Master the basics before unlocking the advanced tools.**

---

## Cash Account Rules (Webull)

### What You CAN Do
- Buy and sell stocks with settled cash
- Buy long calls
- Buy long puts
- Sell covered calls (on shares you own)
- Sell cash-secured puts (if you have the full cash to cover)
- Trade crypto (settles instantly)

### What You CAN'T Do
- Spreads (require margin account)
- Sell naked options
- Short sell stocks
- Use unsettled funds to open new positions

### Settlement Rules
- **Stocks:** T+1 settlement (buy Monday, cash settles Tuesday)
- **Options:** T+1 settlement
- **Crypto:** T+0 (instant settlement — trade all day)
- **Good faith violation:** using unsettled funds to buy AND sell = violation. 3 violations in 12 months = account restricted.

### Day Trading — UNLIMITED
- **Cash account = NO PDT restriction.** PDT only applies to margin accounts.
- You can day trade as many times as you want, as long as you use SETTLED cash.
- Options settle T+1 — so if you sell an option today, that cash is available tomorrow.
- Crypto settles T+0 — trade all day, every day, unlimited.
- **The only limit is settled cash.** Plan your trades around settlement.

   - Risk 15-25% per trade on 5/5 conviction
   - Target: 5-15% gains per swing (2-5 days)

2. **Long calls on momentum** — One contract at a time
   - Stock breaks key level with volume
   - Buy 30-45 DTE call, slightly OTM (5-10%)
   - Cost: $100-300 per contract at this account size
   - Target: 50-100% gain on the option
   - This IS your day trade — use 1 of your 3 weekly day trades if it runs same day

3. **Long puts for crash protection** — When VIX is under 15
   - Buy cheap SPY or QQQ puts as lottery tickets
   - $50-100 per contract

## Cash Account Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Good faith violation** — Using unsettled funds. Plan your trades around settlement.
2. **Wasting day trades** — You only get 3 per week. Save them for 5/5 conviction.
3. **Buying options too close to expiration** — Theta eats you alive. Stay 30+ DTE.
4. **Overtrading crypto** — Just because you CAN trade all day doesn't mean you should.
5. **Not keeping cash reserve** — Always have 15-20% in cash for opportunities.
6. **Trying to do spreads** — You can't. Don't try to work around it. Master long options first.

## Settlement Planning

Since cash settles T+1, plan your trades:

| Day | Action | Cash Status |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Monday | Sell crypto profit | Cash available immediately |
| Monday | Sell stock/option | Cash settles Tuesday |
| Tuesday | Buy new stock/option with Monday's proceeds | Settled |
| Tuesday | Sell Tuesday's buy same day | Uses 1 day trade |

**Crypto hack:** If you need to trade but stock cash hasn't settled, trade crypto instead. It settles instantly.
