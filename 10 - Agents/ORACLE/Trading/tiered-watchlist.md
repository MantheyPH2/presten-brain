### HOT List Rules
1. **Maximum 10 names.** If you want to add one and the list is full, you must REMOVE one first. No exceptions.
2. **Every name must have a defined trade plan** — entry, stop, target, position size. No "I like it" entries.
3. **Review every session.** Remove names whose setup has expired, been stopped out, or hit target.
4. **Catalysts must be within 5 business days.** If the catalyst is further out, move to WARM.
5. **Priority order matters.** #1 is your highest-conviction trade. #10 is your lowest. If you can only make 3 trades, take the top 3.

### How Names Get on HOT
- Promoted from WARM when setup triggers (price reaches entry zone + catalyst imminent)
- Scanner hit with immediate actionability (momentum breakout with volume NOW)
- Earnings play within 5 days
- Technical level hit on a high-conviction name

### How Names Get Removed from HOT
- Trade executed and closed (win or loss)
- Setup expired (price moved away, catalyst passed without trigger)
- Thesis invalidated (news changed the fundamental picture)
- Demoted to WARM (catalyst delayed, setup not ready yet)

## Watchlist Maintenance Checklist

### Every Session (HOT only)
- [ ] Review all HOT positions — any stops hit? Targets reached?
- [ ] Check scanner for new HOT candidates
- [ ] Update entry/stop/target if price action changes the setup

### Every Day (end of day)
- [ ] Review WARM list — any triggers fired?
- [ ] Promote/demote between HOT and WARM as needed
- [ ] Log any watchlist changes in trading journal

### Every Week (Sunday evening)
- [ ] Full review of all three tiers
- [ ] Review COLD list — any approaching buy zones?
- [ ] Add/remove names based on weekly research
- [ ] Ensure tier limits are not exceeded (10/20/30)
- [ ] Cross-reference with sector rotation model for flow changes
