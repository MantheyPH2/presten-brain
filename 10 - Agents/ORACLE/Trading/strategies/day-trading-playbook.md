# Day Trading Playbook

## Reality Check First

- Only 13% of day traders are profitable after 6 months
- Less than 1% are profitable after 5 years
- 97% of persistent day traders in Brazil lost money (academic study)
- Professional day traders at prop firms average 18% annual returns (top 10%)
- Transaction costs create a massive drag: 100 round trips/month at $8/trade = $9,600/year before you make a penny

**Day trading is the hardest way to trade. If you do it, do it with a massive edge and iron discipline.**

## Intraday Setups

### 1. VWAP Breakout/Reclaim
**The institutional day trade.**
- Pre-market: Identify stocks with unusual pre-market volume (RVOL > 3x)
- At the open: Watch how price interacts with VWAP
- If price opens below VWAP and reclaims it on volume = bullish signal, go long
- If price opens above VWAP and loses it on volume = bearish signal, go short
- Stop: Just below VWAP (for longs) or just above (for shorts)
- Target: Next level (prior day high, premarket high, etc.)

### 2. Opening Range Breakout (ORB)
**Classic intraday setup. 40-60% win rate but profitable due to R:R.**
- Define the opening range: First 15 or 30 minutes of trading
- Wait for price to break above the high or below the low of that range
- Enter on the breakout with stop at the opposite side of the range
- Target: 1.5-2x the range height
- **Filter**: Only take breakouts in the direction of the pre-market bias and VWAP
- Recent data shows breakouts occur ~17% of the time, breakdowns ~16%, and double breaks (both sides hit) ~67%
- **Works best on trending days. Avoid in choppy markets.**

### 3. VWAP Pullback
- Strong directional move in the first hour
- Price pulls back to VWAP on declining volume
- Reversal candle at VWAP (hammer, engulfing)
- Enter with stop below VWAP
- Target: Retest of the day's high/low

### 4. Gap Fill Strategy
- Stock gaps up/down significantly at open (> 2%)
- If the gap is in a stock with no major news, it often fills
- Wait for the first 15 minutes to establish direction
- If price starts filling the gap, trade in that direction
- Target: Complete gap fill
- **Never trade gap fills against major news** (earnings, FDA, etc.)

### 5. Red-to-Green / Green-to-Red
- Stock opens red (below prior close) but reverses to green early in the session
- This signals buyers stepping in aggressively
- Enter when price crosses above prior close (red-to-green)
- Stop below the day's low
- Target: Prior day's high or VWAP extensions

## Key Intraday Times

| Time (EST) | What Happens |
|------------|-------------|
| 4:00-9:30 AM | Pre-market. Low liquidity. Establish watchlist and bias. |
| 9:30-10:00 AM | The open. Maximum volatility. ORB forms here. Most retail traders lose money here trading emotionally. |
| 10:00-10:30 AM | "Second wind." Often the real trend of the day establishes. Best entry zone. |
| 11:00-2:00 PM | Midday chop. Avoid trading. Most false signals happen here. |
| 2:00-4:00 PM | Power hour. Institutions position for close. Second-best trading window. |

## Day Trading Rules

1. **Trade only the first 90 minutes and last 60 minutes.** Midday is a meat grinder.
2. **Maximum 3 trades per day.** Quality over quantity.
3. **Risk 0.5-1% per trade.** Smaller than swing trades because intraday noise is higher.
4. **No overnight holds** unless intentionally converting to a swing trade.
5. **Daily loss limit: 2%.** Hit it and you're done for the day. Walk away.
6. **Cash by close** unless you have a specific thesis for holding.

## Tools Required for Day Trading

- Level 2 / DOM data (real-time order book)
- Time & Sales (tape reading)
- VWAP and volume profile indicators
- Fast execution platform (direct market access preferred)
- Multiple monitors (chart + level 2 + time & sales minimum)
- Reliable internet (backup connection recommended)

## The Day Trader's Edge

The only way to be a profitable day trader consistently:
1. **Information edge**: See something others don't (unusual flow, order book patterns)
2. **Speed edge**: Execute faster than the crowd
3. **Discipline edge**: Strict rules, no revenge trading, daily loss limits
4. **Statistical edge**: A strategy with a proven, backtested edge over 200+ trades

You need at least 2 of these 4.
