## Core Philosophy

1. **The market is a professional game played against your emotions.** My edge is discipline, not intelligence.
2. **Risk management IS the strategy.** Everything else is secondary.
3. **Swing trading is the optimal style for risk-adjusted returns.** The statistics overwhelmingly favor it over day trading for retail traders.
4. **Trade with smart money, never against it.** SMC/Wyckoff/institutional flow analysis provides the lens.
5. **Volume confirms everything.** If volume doesn't support the move, the move is suspect.
6. **Less is more.** 2-3 high-quality trades per week beats 20 mediocre ones.

## Market Analysis Framework (Top-Down)

### Step 1: Macro Context (Weekly)
- Check SPY/QQQ/BTC weekly charts. What's the trend?
- Check VIX. Below 15 = calm (mean reversion works). 15-25 = normal (trend following works). Above 25 = volatile (reduce size, widen stops).
- Check the economic calendar for the week (FOMC, CPI, NFP, earnings).
- Determine market regime: Trending / Ranging / Volatile

### Step 2: Higher Timeframe Analysis (Daily)
- **Wyckoff phase**: Is the market in accumulation, markup, distribution, or markdown?
- **Market structure (SMC)**: Higher highs/higher lows (bullish) or lower highs/lower lows (bearish)?
- **Key levels**: Daily/weekly order blocks, supply/demand zones, Fibonacci levels
- **Volume profile**: Where is the Point of Control? Where are the high/low volume nodes?

### Step 3: Entry Timeframe Analysis (4H/1H)
- Wait for price to reach a key HTF level
- Look for LTF confirmation: CHoCH, BOS, spring/upthrust, reversal candle
- Volume confirmation: Is the move supported by volume?
- Fibonacci alignment: Does the entry coincide with a key Fib level?

## Entry Criteria (ALL Must Be True)

Before I enter ANY trade, these conditions must be met:

- [ ] **1. Higher timeframe trend alignment** -- I'm trading in the direction of the Daily/Weekly trend
- [ ] **2. Key level reached** -- Price is at a significant order block, supply/demand zone, or Fib level
- [ ] **3. Lower timeframe confirmation** -- CHoCH/BOS, reversal candle, or spring/upthrust on 1H/4H
- [ ] **4. Volume supports the thesis** -- RVOL > 1.5x, or volume declining on pullback (no supply/no demand)
- [ ] **5. Risk/reward is 1.5:1 minimum** -- Measured from entry to stop and entry to target
- [ ] **6. Portfolio heat check** -- Total open risk is below 5% of account
- [ ] **7. Emotional check** -- I am calm, not revenge trading, not FOMO-ing, not bored

**If any checkbox is unchecked, I DO NOT TRADE.**

## Exit Criteria

### Take Profit
- **Level 1 (1.5-2R)**: Take 1/3 off. Move stop to breakeven.
- **Level 2 (3R)**: Take another 1/3 off. Trail stop below last swing low.
- **Level 3 (structure-based)**: Let final 1/3 ride to the next major supply/demand zone.

### Cut Loss
- **Stop loss hit**: Exit immediately. No moving stops further away. Ever.
- **Time stop**: If the trade hasn't moved in my direction after 3-5 days, exit and reassess.
- **Thesis invalidated**: If the reason I entered is no longer valid (structure breaks, volume diverges), exit regardless of P&L.

## Position Sizing Rules

### Standard Sizing
- Risk per trade: **1% of account**
- Formula: Position Size = (Account x 0.01) / (Entry - Stop Loss)
- Maximum concurrent positions: **5** (5% total portfolio heat)

### Adjusted Sizing
- High conviction (4/4 confluence factors): **1.5%** risk
- Uncertain market / elevated VIX: **0.5%** risk
- Crypto positions: **0.5-1%** risk (higher volatility = smaller size)
- Meme coins: **0.25%** risk maximum

### Drawdown Scaling
| Account Drawdown | Position Size Adjustment |
|-----------------|------------------------|
| 0-5% | Full size (1%) |
| 5-10% | Half size (0.5%) |
| 10-15% | Quarter size (0.25%) |
| 15%+ | Stop trading. Full review. |

## Strategy Selection by Market Condition

### Trending Market (VIX 12-22, clear direction)
- **Primary**: Momentum + SMC pullback entries
- **Secondary**: Swing trade breakouts
- **Options**: Bull/bear credit spreads in trend direction

### Range-Bound Market (VIX 12-18, no clear direction)
- **Primary**: Mean reversion at range extremes
- **Secondary**: Iron condors for options income
- **Options**: Wheel strategy on quality stocks

### High Volatility (VIX > 25)
- **Primary**: Reduce trading frequency. Only A+ setups.
- **Secondary**: Sell premium (credit spreads) -- IV is elevated
- **Caution**: Wider stops needed. Reduce position size by 50%.
- **Opportunity**: Wyckoff spring/shakeout plays at capitulation levels

### Crypto Bull Market
- **Primary**: Momentum + on-chain confirmation (exchange flows, funding rates)
- **Secondary**: Funding rate arbitrage for passive income
- **Degen allocation**: 1-2% total portfolio in high-conviction meme plays

## Daily Trading Routine

### Pre-Market (7:00-9:00 AM)
1. Review overnight action on BTC, ES futures, global markets
2. Check economic calendar -- any events today?
3. Review watchlist (5-10 names max)
4. Update key levels on daily charts
5. Set alerts at key levels (don't stare at charts)
6. Emotional check: Am I in the right headspace to trade?

### Market Hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM)
- **9:30-10:30 AM**: Watch, don't trade. Let the opening noise settle.
- **10:30 AM**: Assess if any setups are triggering. Enter if criteria met.
- **11:00 AM - 2:00 PM**: Avoid new entries. Manage existing positions.
- **2:00-4:00 PM**: Second window for entries (power hour).
- **Maximum 2-3 new trades per day.**

### Post-Market (4:00-5:00 PM)
1. Journal every trade taken today (entry, exit, emotional state, rule adherence)
2. Review open positions -- any stops to adjust?
3. Scan for tomorrow's setups
4. Log today's P&L and portfolio heat

### Weekend (30-60 min)
1. Weekly chart review (SPY, QQQ, BTC, DXY, VIX)
2. Review all trades from the week -- patterns in wins and losses?
3. Assess market regime for next week
4. Build next week's watchlist (5-10 names)

## Rules I Will NEVER Break

1. **Every trade has a stop loss placed BEFORE entry.** No exceptions.
2. **Maximum 1% risk per trade.** No "just this once" with bigger size.
3. **Maximum 5% portfolio heat.** If I'm at 5%, the next trade waits.
4. **Daily loss limit: 2%.** Hit it = done for the day. Close the platform.
5. **No revenge trading.** 15-minute mandatory cooldown after any loss.
6. **No FOMO entries.** If I didn't plan the trade, I don't take the trade.
7. **Never average down** on a losing position. Add only to winners.
8. **Never move a stop further from entry.** Only tighten, never widen.
9. **Exit at 50% profit on all options income trades.** Greed kills sellers.
10. **Journal every trade.** No trade without a journal entry.
11. **No trading when emotional.** Angry, tired, distracted, euphoric = no trading.
12. **Weekly review is mandatory.** Skip it and you repeat mistakes forever.
