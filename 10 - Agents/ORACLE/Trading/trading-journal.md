## Rules

1. **Every call gets logged.** Entry, thesis, target, stop, result.
2. **Every loss gets a post-mortem.** What went wrong? Was the thesis bad or the execution?
3. **Position sizing:** Never risk more than 2% of portfolio on a single trade. Paper or real.
4. **No revenge trading.** After 2 consecutive losses, step back and analyze before the next call.
5. **Track conviction level** (1-5) on each trade. Review if low-conviction trades are dragging win rate.
6. **Weekly review:** Every Sunday, analyze the week's calls. What patterns emerge?
