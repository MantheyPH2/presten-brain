# Post-Trade Review Template

**Complete this within 24 hours of closing every position. No exceptions. The trade is not finished until the review is written.**

---

## Why This Exists

P&L tells you what happened. The review tells you why. Without the review, every losing trade is just a loss. With the review, every losing trade is either a lesson or confirmation that you followed a good process that had a bad outcome — and those are completely different things.

Traders who review every trade improve. Traders who only review big wins and big losses stay the same.
