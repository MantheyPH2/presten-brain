## Why This Exists

The weekly review is where patterns become visible. A single bad trade is noise. Three bad trades in the same scenario is a pattern. The review turns noise into signal — and that signal is the actual product of a week's work, more valuable than any individual trade.

Traders who skip the weekly review are doomed to repeat the same mistakes because they never see them clearly. Traders who complete it compound their edge over time.
