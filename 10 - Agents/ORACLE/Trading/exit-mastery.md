# Exit Mastery

**Entries are easy. Exits make you rich or broke.**

Most traders obsess over entries and wing their exits. That's backwards. A mediocre entry with a perfect exit beats a perfect entry with a panic exit every time.

---

## The Three Exit Decisions

Every trade reaches a point where you face one of three choices:

### 1. Take Full Profit (close everything)
### 2. Scale Out (sell part, let the rest ride)
### 3. Let It Ride (hold everything, trail the stop)

**The right answer depends on the SITUATION, not a fixed rule.**

---

## Decision Framework

Ask these questions in order:

### Question 1: Has the thesis been fully realized?
- **YES → Take full profit.** The trade did what you expected. Don't get greedy waiting for more. Book it.
  - Example: You bought AMZN calls for earnings. Earnings happened, stock popped, thesis complete. Sell.
- **NO, still playing out → Move to Question 2**
  - Example: You bought BTC at $74K for the funding rate squeeze. It's at $78K but funding is still negative. Thesis isn't fully played out yet.

### Question 2: Is there a NEW catalyst ahead that could move it further?
- **YES → Scale out, let rest ride.**
  - Sell 30-50% to lock in guaranteed profit
  - Let 50-70% ride into the next catalyst with a trailing stop
  - Example: BTC at $78K, you're up 5.4%. April 29 mega-cap earnings could push risk-on higher. Scale out 30%, trail the rest.
- **NO further catalysts → Take full profit.**
  - No reason to hold = no reason to hope
  - Example: Stock ran 15% on news. No earnings coming. No more catalysts. Take the money.

### Question 3: How extended is the move?
- **Parabolic (up 20%+ in days, RSI >80) → Take 70-100%.** These always pull back. Lock it in.
- **Steady grind (up 5-10% over weeks) → Scale out 30-50%, trail rest.** Healthy trend, could continue.
- **Just started (up 1-3%) → Let it ride.** Too early to take profit. Move stop to breakeven.

## The Scaling System

### The 30/30/40 Rule (for swing trades)
When a trade is working:

| Milestone | Action | Reasoning |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **+5% to +10%** | Move stop to breakeven | You can't lose money now. Free trade. |
| **+10% to +20%** | Sell 30% | Lock in guaranteed profit. Reduce risk. |
| **+20% to +50%** | Sell another 30% (60% total sold) | Serious money banked. House money on the rest. |
| **+50%+** | Trail remaining 40% with a 15-20% trailing stop | Let the runner run. This is where 10-baggers come from. |

### For Options (decay faster, need tighter management)

| Milestone | Action | Reasoning |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **+30% on the option** | Move mental stop to entry (if option pulls back to what you paid, exit) | |
| **+50%** | Sell 50% | Options can reverse 50% in a day. Lock it. |
| **+100% (doubled)** | Sell 50% of remaining (75% total sold) | You've recovered your entire investment. Rest is pure profit. |
| **+200%+** | Trail remaining 25% aggressively (30% trailing stop on the option value) | |
| **+500%+** | Sell everything or keep 10% as a lottery ticket | This is life-changing territory. Don't give it back. |

### For Crypto (higher volatility, wider stops)

| Milestone | Action | Reasoning |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **+10%** | Move stop to breakeven | Crypto can reverse 10% in an hour |
| **+20%** | Sell 25% | |
| **+40%** | Sell another 25% (50% total) | |
| **+75%+** | Sell another 25% (75% total) | |
| **+100%+** | Trail final 25% with 25% trailing stop | Moon bag. If it goes to 0, you already banked 75%. |

## When to Let It Ride (DON'T sell)

Not every winning trade should be sold. These are the times to HOLD:

### 1. Trend is accelerating
- Volume increasing on up days
- Higher highs getting bigger
- RSI staying above 50 but not overbought
- Action: trail stop, don't touch it

### 2. You're in early on a multi-week thesis
- You bought the crash recovery trade on day 1
- The recovery historically takes 4-8 weeks
- You're on week 1 with a 10% gain
- Action: you're not even close to done. Hold. Scale out only at 30%+.

### 3. A bigger catalyst is still ahead
- You bought before earnings and the stock ran 5% pre-earnings
- Earnings hasn't happened yet
- Action: don't sell the pre-run. The catalyst is the whole point.

### 4. The position is small relative to your account
- You have 3% of your account in this trade
- It's up 40%
- Selling it gives you 1.2% account gain
- Holding it with a trailing stop could give you 5%+ account gain
- Action: the risk/reward of holding a small winning position is almost always favorable

## When to Take Everything Off (SELL NOW)

### 1. Thesis is dead
- You bought BTC for the funding rate squeeze. Funding went positive. Thesis invalidated.
- **Don't wait for the stop. Exit immediately.** The reason you entered no longer exists.

### 2. Parabolic blow-off
- Stock/crypto went vertical. Up 30%+ in 2-3 days. RSI above 85. Volume spiking.
- **This ALWAYS pulls back.** Take 70-100% off. You can re-enter lower.

### 3. Major regime change
- Market was bullish, now VIX is spiking and your crash risk scorecard jumped from 2 to 6
- **Reduce everything.** The environment changed. Your positions were sized for a different regime.

### 4. Pre-event de-risk
- You have 5 long positions going into a binary event (FOMC, CPI, geopolitical)
- You don't know the outcome
- **Sell 30-50% across the board before the event.** Re-enter after if conditions are favorable.

### 5. You've hit your target
- You planned this trade with a specific target price. It hit the target.
- **Take the money.** Don't move the target because "it might go higher." That's greed talking. Your plan was right. Execute it.

## The Trailing Stop System

Once a trade is profitable and you're letting it ride:

### For Stocks
- **Tight trail (strong conviction, in a trend):** 7-10% below current price
- **Normal trail:** 10-15% below current price
- **Loose trail (volatile stock, long timeframe):** 15-20% below current price
- Adjust the trail every time the stock makes a new high. Never lower a trailing stop.

### For Options
- **Trail based on option value, not stock price**
- If option is worth $10 and you want a 30% trail: stop at $7
- Recalculate every session as the option value changes
- Options move faster than stocks — trails get hit more often

### For Crypto
- **Trail 15-25% in normal conditions** (crypto is volatile)
- **Trail 10-15% in strong trend** (momentum is clear)
- **Trail 30%+ for small "moon bag" positions** (let it run or die)
- Use the 4hr chart for trailing — not the 1min (noise will stop you out)

## The Hardest Decisions

### "It's up 50% but I think it can go higher"
- **Scale out.** Sell 50%. If it goes higher, you still have 50% riding. If it reverses, you banked real money. This is ALWAYS the right play. You eliminate regret in both directions.

### "It hit my stop but I think it'll bounce"
- **Get out.** Your stop exists for a reason. You set it when you were rational. Now you're emotional. Trust the plan you made when you were thinking clearly.

### "It's flat for 2 weeks — should I sell?"
- **Yes, if the catalyst has passed.** Time is money. Capital sitting in a flat trade can't compound in a better one.
- **No, if the catalyst hasn't happened yet.** Patience is the trade. Don't exit a thesis before it plays out.

### "I'm up 300% — when do I sell?"
- **You should have already scaled out at 50% and 100%.** If you didn't, sell 75% RIGHT NOW. Keep 25% as a moon bag with a 30% trailing stop. You've already won. Don't turn a 300% win into a 50% win by being greedy.

## ORACLE's Exit Rules (Summary)

1. **Always have an exit plan BEFORE entering.** Target, stop, and scaling plan written down.
2. **Move stop to breakeven once up 5-10%.** Never let a winner become a loser.
3. **Scale out in tranches.** Don't sell 100% at once unless thesis is dead or move is parabolic.
4. **Trail stops, never lower them.** The stop only moves in your favor.
5. **Sell the plan, not the emotion.** If your target was $82K, sell at $82K. Don't change it to $85K because you're excited.
6. **Time is a cost.** If a trade isn't working in 3-5 days, reassess. Dead money is opportunity cost.
7. **Respect parabolic moves.** Up 30%+ in 3 days = take most off. It WILL pull back.
8. **Keep a moon bag on your best trades.** 10-25% trailing with a wide stop. This is where 10x returns come from.
9. **Thesis dead = exit immediately.** Don't wait for the stop. The reason is gone.
10. **Post every exit publicly.** Win or loss. The accountability sharpens the discipline.
