### Funding Rate Thesis
This is the most important technical signal in crypto right now:

**47+ consecutive days of negative funding on Binance.**

What this means mechanically:
- Shorts are paying longs to keep their positions open
- This indicates overwhelming bearish positioning by leveraged traders (primarily retail)
- When the market turns, these shorts get liquidated, creating a cascading buy-side force (short squeeze)

Historical precedents:
- Jan 2023: 35 days of negative funding preceded a 45% rally from $16K to $23K
- Oct 2023: 28 days of negative funding preceded the move from $27K to $44K
- Current: 47+ days — the most extreme in BTC's history

**ORACLE's thesis:** The short squeeze has not been triggered because macro headwinds (Iran, Warsh) are preventing the catalyst. But the spring is coiled. When macro clears — even partially — the squeeze will be violent and fast. The move could take BTC from $74K to $82K+ in 48-72 hours.

### Scenarios
**Bull:** Iran resolves Wednesday → BTC short squeeze fires → $74K to $80K in 48 hours → $85K within 2 weeks. Probability: 30%.

**Neutral:** Iran unresolved, macro chops → BTC ranges $72K-$76K → waits for Apr 29 earnings as next catalyst. Probability: 40%.

**Bear:** Iran escalates OR BTC breaks $70K → cascading liquidations on longs → $65K test. Probability: 30%.

- 30-day rolling correlation: approximately -0.6 to -0.8
- Strong dollar = BTC headwind. Both Warsh hawkishness and Iran safe-haven flows strengthen the dollar.
- When Iran resolves → dollar weakens → BTC gets a tailwind from two directions (risk-on + weaker dollar)
- This is why the Iran resolution scenario is so bullish for BTC — it removes both the geopolitical risk AND the dollar headwind simultaneously

- The Apr 29 earnings are the catalyst for the rotation back IF the numbers are strong
