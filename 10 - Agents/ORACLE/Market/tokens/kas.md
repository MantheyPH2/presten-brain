## What It Does
Kaspa is a Proof-of-Work blockchain that uses the GHOSTDAG protocol — a generalization of the Nakamoto consensus that allows blocks created in parallel to coexist and be ordered in consensus. This solves the classic PoW trilemma: Kaspa achieves 1-second block times and high throughput while maintaining full PoW security. No other PoW chain comes close to this speed. Kaspa has no smart contracts yet (in development), no pre-mine, no VC funding, and fair launch — similar to Bitcoin's ethos but with modern technology.
