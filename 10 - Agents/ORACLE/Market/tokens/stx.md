## What It Does
Stacks is a Bitcoin Layer 2 that brings smart contracts and DeFi to Bitcoin without modifying Bitcoin's base layer. It uses Proof of Transfer (PoX) consensus — miners spend BTC to mine STX blocks, and STX stakers earn BTC rewards. This creates a unique economic link between BTC and STX. The Nakamoto upgrade (2024) dramatically improved Stacks by introducing fast blocks (~5 seconds vs previous 10-30 minutes) and 100% Bitcoin finality. Key use cases: Bitcoin DeFi (sBTC allows trustless BTC-backed DeFi), Bitcoin NFTs, Bitcoin-secured dApps.
