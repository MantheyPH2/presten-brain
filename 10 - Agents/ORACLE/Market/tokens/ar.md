## What It Does
Arweave is a decentralized permanent storage protocol. Unlike Filecoin (which charges ongoing fees), Arweave uses a one-time payment model — pay once, store forever. The protocol achieves this through an endowment-based economic model where storage fees include a projected future cost premium. Arweave also created AO (Arweave Operating System) — a hyper-parallel computing environment built on top of permanent storage. AO enables unlimited parallel processes, creating a "world computer" with permanent data storage.

## Why Track This
- **Permanent storage moat:** "Pay once, store forever" is a unique value proposition. No other protocol offers truly permanent data storage
- **AO compute platform:** AO transforms Arweave from a storage protocol into a computing platform — massively expanding the TAM
- **AI + permanent data:** AI models need permanent, verifiable training data. Arweave's permaweb provides this — provenance and permanence for AI datasets
- **Small cap, big thesis:** At ~$120M market cap, AR has massive upside if AO gains developer traction
- **Differentiated from Filecoin:** Arweave (permanent storage, one-time fee) vs Filecoin (temporary storage, recurring fees) — different use cases, different markets
