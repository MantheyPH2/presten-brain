## What It Does
Immutable is a Layer 2 scaling solution for NFTs and blockchain gaming on Ethereum. It uses zkEVM (zero-knowledge Ethereum Virtual Machine) technology to provide gas-free minting and trading of NFTs with Ethereum-level security. The platform includes: Immutable Passport (gamer onboarding), Immutable Orderbook (NFT marketplace infrastructure), and Immutable zkEVM (the L2 chain). Major game partnerships include Illuvium, Gods Unchained, Guild of Guardians, Metalcore, and others. Immutable is THE infrastructure play for blockchain gaming.
