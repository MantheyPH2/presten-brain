## What It Does
Pendle is a yield tokenization protocol. It takes yield-bearing assets (like stETH, aUSDC, GLP) and splits them into two components: PT (Principal Token — represents the underlying asset redeemable at maturity) and YT (Yield Token — represents all future yield generated by the asset between now and maturity). Users can: (1) Buy PT at a discount to lock in a fixed yield, (2) Buy YT to speculate on yield going up, (3) Provide liquidity to earn swap fees. This creates a derivatives market for DeFi yields — essentially an interest rate derivatives protocol for crypto. Pendle has become an essential tool for sophisticated DeFi users managing yield exposure, and has benefited enormously from the liquid staking and restaking narratives.
