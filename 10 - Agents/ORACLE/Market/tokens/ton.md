## What It Does
Toncoin is the native token of The Open Network (TON), a Layer-1 blockchain originally developed by Telegram. After Telegram stepped back due to SEC pressure, the open-source community took over development. TON's killer advantage is Telegram integration — with 900M+ Telegram users, TON has the largest potential onboarding funnel of any blockchain. The chain uses a multi-chain architecture with dynamic sharding for scalability. Key use cases: Telegram mini-apps, in-app payments, TON DNS, TON Storage, and DeFi.

## Why Track This
- **Telegram distribution moat:** No other blockchain has access to 900M+ users through a native app integration. TON Wallet is built into Telegram
- **Mini-app ecosystem:** Telegram mini-apps (games, DeFi, social) are onboarding millions of non-crypto-native users
- **Advertising revenue:** TON is used for Telegram's ad marketplace — real utility driving demand
- **Massive user base, low market cap:** At $3.4B, TON is undervalued relative to its user reach if adoption metrics continue growing
- **Regulatory risk:** Pavel Durov's legal issues in France and SEC history create headline risk
