## What It Does
FLOKI started as a meme coin (named after Elon Musk's Shiba Inu dog) but has evolved into a utility-focused project. Key products: Valhalla (P2E metaverse game), FlokiFi (DeFi suite including FlokiFi Locker for token liquidity locking), and Floki University (crypto education platform). FLOKI is on both Ethereum and BNB Chain. The project has pivoted from pure meme to "meme with utility" — trying to bridge the gap between Dogecoin-style community and real product development.
