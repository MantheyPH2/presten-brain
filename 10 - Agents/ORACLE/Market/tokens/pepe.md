# Pepe (PEPE)

## Current State
**Price:** $0.0000037–$0.0000039
**Trend:** Range-bound at low levels. Thin volume. Quiet.

## What It Does
PEPE is an Ethereum-based meme coin based on the Pepe the Frog internet meme. Launched in April 2023, it was one of the fastest meme coins to reach significant market cap, driven by the cultural resonance of the Pepe meme (which predates crypto). PEPE has no utility, no roadmap, no development team. It is a pure cultural/speculative asset. Its advantage over other meme coins is the age and universality of the Pepe meme itself — Pepe the Frog has been an internet staple since the mid-2000s. PEPE is an ERC-20 token on Ethereum.

## Bull Case
- PEPE has the most culturally universal meme base — Pepe the Frog has global recognition
- It's Ethereum-based — accessible to the broadest crypto audience
- In any meme season, PEPE historically receives significant capital flows
- The $0.0000037 range represents a significant drawdown from ATH — mean reversion potential in a bull market

## Bear Case
- Meme sector structurally damaged — 75% decline may indicate the supercycle is over, not just pausing
- No utility, no development, no defensible moat
- New meme coins constantly launching, competing for the finite pool of speculative capital
- Retail investors burned in the 2024–2025 cycle are slower to return

## ORACLE's Take
PEPE is the strongest meme coin after DOGE based purely on cultural longevity of the underlying meme. In any meme season revival, PEPE would be a top performer. But "meme season revival" is not a catalyst you can time — it requires broad retail enthusiasm.
