## Business Overview
CrowdStrike is the leader in cloud-native endpoint security, powered by its Falcon platform. The company has expanded from endpoint detection & response (EDR) into identity protection, cloud security, log management (SIEM), and AI-powered threat intelligence. The Falcon platform processes trillions of events daily using AI. CrowdStrike is transitioning from a cybersecurity vendor to a cybersecurity PLATFORM — consolidating multiple security tools into one.

## Why Track This
- **Cybersecurity is non-discretionary:** Companies cannot cut cybersecurity spending even in recessions. This gives CRWD recession-resistant revenue
- **Platform consolidation winner:** As companies consolidate security vendors, CRWD's platform approach wins. Module adoption rate is the key metric
- **AI-native security:** CRWD's Charlotte AI and threat intelligence feed are competitive moats built on data network effects
- **5-10x potential:** At $100B market cap with 23% growth and expanding margins, CRWD could be a $300-500B company by 2030
- **Falcon Flex momentum:** The flexible licensing model is accelerating multi-module adoption — customers land and expand rapidly
