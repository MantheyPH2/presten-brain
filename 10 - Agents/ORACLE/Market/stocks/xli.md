## Business Overview
XLI tracks the Industrial Select Sector Index — all S&P 500 industrial companies. Includes aerospace & defense, machinery, construction, transportation, and electrical equipment. This is the "real economy" sector — factories, infrastructure, defense spending, and logistics. Highly correlated with GDP growth, PMI data, and government infrastructure spending.

## Why Track This
- **Economic cycle indicator:** Industrials are the purest GDP-beta play. When ISM Manufacturing PMI is above 50, XLI outperforms. Below 50, it lags
- **Infrastructure spending:** The CHIPS Act, infrastructure bill, and reshoring trend create multi-year tailwinds for industrial names
- **Defense spending:** Geopolitical tensions (Ukraine, Taiwan, Middle East) drive defense budget expansion — RTX, Lockheed, Northrop are major holdings
- **Reshoring play:** Manufacturing returning to the US benefits Caterpillar, Deere, and equipment makers
- **Leading indicator:** Industrials tend to peak 3-6 months before recessions and bottom 3-6 months before recoveries
