## Business Overview
Coinbase is the largest regulated cryptocurrency exchange in the US. Revenue mix: Transaction revenue (~60% — trading fees), Subscription & Services (~35% — staking, custody, USDC interest, Coinbase One), Other (~5%). The company has evolved from a pure exchange into a crypto financial services conglomerate — exchange, custody, staking, stablecoin (USDC partnership with Circle), derivatives, and now potentially banking. The OCC bank charter is a game-changer if fully approved.

## Why Track This
- **Crypto beta with equity market access:** COIN is the easiest way to get leveraged crypto exposure in a brokerage account. It moves 1.5-2x BTC on average
- **Regulatory moat:** As the only major US exchange with S&P 500 membership and OCC banking approval, Coinbase has a regulatory moat that competitors cannot easily replicate
- **USDC story:** USDC is a revenue machine — Coinbase earns interest on USDC reserves. Higher rates = more USDC revenue
- **Derivatives expansion:** The crypto options exchange acquisition positions COIN for the next phase of crypto market maturation
- **Crypto cycle amplifier:** COIN outperforms in bull markets and underperforms in bear markets by 2-3x. Know the cycle, know when to own COIN
