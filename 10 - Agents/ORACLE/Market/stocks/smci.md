## Business Overview
Super Micro designs and manufactures high-performance server systems optimized for AI workloads, cloud computing, and data centers. The company's "Building Block" architecture allows rapid customization of server configurations, making them a preferred partner for companies deploying NVIDIA GPU clusters. SMCI is a key enabler of AI infrastructure — they build the physical servers that run AI training and inference workloads. However, the company has faced governance and accounting concerns that have weighed heavily on the stock.

## Why Track This
- **AI infrastructure picks-and-shovels play:** SMCI builds the servers that house NVIDIA GPUs. Every AI data center expansion benefits SMCI
- **High-risk/high-reward momentum play:** SMCI has been one of the most volatile stocks in the market — it went from $10 to $120 and back to $26. This kind of volatility creates trading opportunities
- **Governance risk = discount:** The accounting investigation and delisting risk created a massive discount to fair value. If governance clears, rerating potential is significant
- **NVIDIA ecosystem:** SMCI is deeply integrated into NVIDIA's supply chain. NVIDIA GPU demand = SMCI revenue
- **Small cap in a mega-cap sector:** Unlike NVDA ($3T), SMCI ($16B) can move 50%+ on a single catalyst
