## Business Overview
Alphabet's revenue is ~75% advertising (Search + YouTube), ~13% Google Cloud, and the rest across hardware, subscriptions, and Other Bets (Waymo, DeepMind, Verily). Google Search remains the most profitable digital business ever built. Google Cloud is the #3 cloud provider but growing fastest among the major three.

## Bull Case
- Search advertising is structurally durable — AI Overviews haven't cannibalized monetization
- Google Cloud at 28% growth with AI workloads (TPUs, Vertex AI) is undervalued
- Waymo is worth $100B+ independently — autonomous vehicles real and deployed
- Gemini is genuinely competitive with GPT-4o and Claude — not a laggard
- Valuation: ~22x forward earnings is cheap for a company with Search, Cloud, YouTube, Waymo
- $70B buyback program ongoing

## Bear Case
- DOJ antitrust: if Google is forced to divest Chrome, default search deals, or Android — catastrophic
- AI chatbots (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) are a structural headwind to Search query volume
- Cloud is #3 and must spend aggressively ($75B+ capex 2026) to close gap with AWS/Azure
- YouTube facing competition from TikTok, Shorts cannibalization of long-form CPMs
- OpenAI SearchGPT gaining share in query market — existential threat if it scales
