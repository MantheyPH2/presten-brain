## Business Overview
Tesla operates across Automotive (Model 3/Y/S/X/Cybertruck), Energy Generation & Storage (Megapack, Powerwall), and Services. The longer-term thesis revolves on Full Self-Driving (FSD), Robotaxi (Cybercab launch), Optimus humanoid robot, and energy storage scaling. Elon Musk's political role remains a significant brand/demand risk factor.
