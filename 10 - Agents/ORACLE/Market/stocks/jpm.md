## Business Overview
JPMorgan is the largest US bank by assets (~$4T). Revenue mix: Consumer & Community Banking (~40%), Corporate & Investment Bank (~35%), Commercial Banking (~10%), Asset & Wealth Management (~15%). The bank is a universal financial institution — retail banking, credit cards, investment banking, trading, asset management. Jamie Dimon is widely considered the best bank CEO in the world. JPM consistently outperforms peers on ROE, efficiency ratio, and revenue growth.

## Why Track This
- **Financial system bellwether:** JPM is THE bank. Its earnings set the tone for the entire financial sector every quarter
- **Economic indicator:** JPM's loan loss provisions, consumer spending data, and credit card delinquency rates are real-time economic data
- **Jamie Dimon factor:** Dimon's annual shareholder letter and public commentary move markets. His succession is a key risk/catalyst
- **Trading revenue:** JPM's FICC and Equities trading desks are the largest on Wall Street. Volatility = trading revenue = JPM outperformance
- **Dividend aristocrat:** Consistent dividend growth, currently ~2.3% yield with buybacks
