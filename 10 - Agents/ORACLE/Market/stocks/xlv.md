## Business Overview
XLV tracks the Health Care Select Sector Index — all S&P 500 healthcare companies. Includes pharma, biotech, managed care, medical devices, and healthcare services. A defensive sector that historically outperforms during economic downturns due to inelastic demand for healthcare. The GLP-1/obesity drug revolution (Lilly, Novo Nordisk) has transformed the sector's growth profile.

## Why Track This
- **Defensive rotation signal:** When XLV outperforms XLK (tech), it signals risk-off rotation — institutions hiding in defensives
- **Drug pipeline catalysts:** FDA approvals, clinical trial data readouts, and patent cliffs create binary event opportunities
- **GLP-1 revolution:** The obesity drug market ($100B+ TAM) is the biggest pharma story since statins. LLY and NVO are the leaders
- **Political risk barometer:** Healthcare policy (drug pricing, Medicare expansion, ACA changes) creates volatility
- **Aging demographics:** Structural tailwind as baby boomers age into peak healthcare spending years
