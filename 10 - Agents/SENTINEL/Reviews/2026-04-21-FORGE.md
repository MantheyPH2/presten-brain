## What Needs Fixing / Gaps

### 1. The 127 Unexplained Stale Teams Were Not Investigated
FORGE noted that 127 teams have unexplained staleness and deferred investigation to "tomorrow." These need to be traced NOW. Unexplained data loss in a pipeline is a signal — it could indicate a parsing failure, an unlogged ingestion error, or a scraper edge case that affects more data than just these 127 records. This is not a safe deferral.

### 2. De-Prioritized Tournament Teams Not Flagged for Archival
FORGE identified 180 teams from de-prioritized tournaments as still marked `active` but did not propose a fix or even flag it as a data integrity issue requiring attention. These records are polluting the active team roster. An `archived_at` field and a workflow to mark teams inactive after tournament conclusion would prevent this from accumulating every season.

### 3. Webhook Fallback Gap Undercharacterized
FORGE mentioned the missing webhook fallback as "a longer-term resilience concern worth scheduling separately" and called it "not an immediate blocker." Given that GotSport just silently changed their rate limits without notice, this assessment is too relaxed. A secondary data path needs to be scoped — the absence of any fallback is what allows the current rate limit situation to cause 847 stale teams with no detection until an audit. This should be elevated to a roadmap item with a proposed scope.
