## What Needs Fixing / Gaps

ELO flagged U13/U14 calibration as a "concern" and a "mild" issue for U12 and U19. SENTINEL's independent check of the Brier scores shows this is more serious:

- U13 Brier score: **0.31** (acceptable threshold for this domain: ≤ 0.23)
- U14 Brier score: **0.27** (still outside acceptable range)

ELO's phrasing ("warrant validation," "flagged for SENTINEL's review") is too soft for the actual severity. A Brier score of 0.31 means the model's win probability predictions are meaningfully worse than a coin flip adjusted for base rates. This is not a "let's validate before acting" situation — it is a confirmed miscalibration that needs a fix schedule.

### 2. Brier Score Analysis Was Framed as Parallel to the Transition Script
ELO's briefing lists the Brier score analysis (Task 3) and the age group transition scope (Task 4) as concurrent work. They are NOT concurrent. The Brier score analysis must complete and calibration values must be corrected BEFORE the transition migration script is finalized. If the transition script carries forward ratings derived from a miscalibrated model, every team that passes through U13/U14 will start 2026-27 with systematically biased ratings. This cascade will take a full season to self-correct under Glicko-2's natural RD decay.

**Flag: calibration-review-needed — This must be treated as a prerequisite, not parallel work.**

### 3. MLS NEXT Two-Tier Structure Not Reflected in Calibration Analysis
ELO's calibration table review covered per-age-group parameters but did not address the MLS NEXT two-tier split (Homegrown Tier 1 vs Academy Tier 2) documented in Recent Changes 2024-2026. Currently the ranking engine applies 160 calibration points to all MLS NEXT teams regardless of whether they are Homegrown or Academy tier. This is a known gap that ELO should analyze and produce a calibration recommendation for. The two tiers have meaningfully different competitive levels and the current uniform treatment inflates Academy team ratings relative to their actual competition level.
