### Methodology Comparison vs USA Rank

Key divergences identified in ELO's work:
- **USA Rank uses last 20 games in last year; we use full history.** For teams with strong recent form and poor historical records, USA Rank will rank them higher. This is a feature gap for coaches tracking in-season momentum.
- **USA Rank uses goal difference; we use win/loss/draw.** High-scoring teams with dominant margins will rank higher on USA Rank than on Evo Draw. This could explain divergence for certain club styles.
- **USA Rank's league calibration is emergent; ours is hardcoded.** Hardcoded calibration is more controllable but potentially less accurate for leagues we have limited cross-league data for.

The ranking accuracy audit (ELO task) will surface whether these divergences are producing materially different rankings for real, known elite teams.
