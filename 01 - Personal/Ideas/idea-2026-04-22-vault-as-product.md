# Idea: "The Operator's Vault" — Sell Your System, Not Your Time

## The One-Liner

Package and sell Presten's Obsidian + AI agent system as a premium product for founders, operators, and builders who want a working "second brain + AI team" system without building it from scratch.

---

## The Core Insight

Presten has spent significant time building an Obsidian vault with:
- A multi-world knowledge architecture (Personal, Evo Draw, etc.)
- A working AI agent network (ORACLE, ELO, FORGE, SENTINEL)
