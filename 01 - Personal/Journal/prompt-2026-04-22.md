# Journal Prompt — April 22, 2026

## Today's Prompt

> **You are building multiple things simultaneously — a company, a vault of knowledge, a financial future, a version of yourself. If you stripped away every obligation, every expectation others have of you, and every project that exists only because of momentum rather than genuine desire — what would you still choose to build? And what does the answer reveal about who you actually are versus who you've been performing?**

---

## Expansion Questions

Use these if the main prompt doesn't unlock anything on its own:

1. When you imagine your best possible week, what kind of work fills it? Is that the work you did last week?

2. There's a version of you five years from now who has "made it" by every external metric — but who is quietly hollow. What did that version trade away? Are you trading any of those same things right now?

3. What project or commitment would you cancel tomorrow if social pressure were removed entirely? What does your hesitation to cancel it tell you?

4. Where in your life are you building for the audience rather than for yourself? Is that a problem, or is it actually fine?

5. What belief about yourself is the loudest blocker between where you are and where you want to be? Is that belief true, or just old?
