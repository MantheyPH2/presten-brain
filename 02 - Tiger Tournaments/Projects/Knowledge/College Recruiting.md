## How Coaches Find Players

Ranked by effectiveness (based on coach surveys and recruiting industry data):

### 1. Tournaments and Showcases (Primary)
- Coaches travel to [[Tournament Landscape|major events]] to watch live play
- MLS NEXT Fest, ECNL Nationals, Dallas Cup, Jefferson Cup = highest attendance
- **This is where 60%+ of initial recruiting contacts are made**

### 2. ID Camps
- College-hosted camps where coaches evaluate players directly
- Players pay to attend (typically $200-500)
- More controlled evaluation environment than tournaments

### 3. League Play
- Coaches watch [[League Hierarchy|top-tier league]] games
- [[MLS NEXT]], [[ECNL]], and GA games get the most attention
- Regular season performance over time is valued

### 4. Highlight Videos
- Short (3-5 min) edited game clips
- Used for initial screening before live evaluation
- Increasingly important but NOT a substitute for live viewing

### 5. Rankings and Data
- Coaches check rankings to identify teams/players to scout
- **This is Evo Draw's entry point** into the recruiting funnel
- National rankings help coaches discover talent outside their region

## Recruiting Timeline

| Grade   | Age   | Activity                                    |
| ------- | ----- | ------------------------------------------- |
| 9th     | 14-15 | Begin attending showcases, build highlight reel |
| 10th    | 15-16 | ID camps, initial coach contact             |
| 11th    | 16-17 | Official/unofficial visits, verbal commits  |
| 12th    | 17-18 | NLI signing (Nov/Feb), final decisions      |
