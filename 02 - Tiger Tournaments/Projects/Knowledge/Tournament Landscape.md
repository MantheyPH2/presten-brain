# Tournament Landscape

Major national youth soccer tournaments and showcases, organized by timing and scout attendance level. These are the events where [[College Recruiting|college coaches]] actively recruit.

---

## Tier 1 — Premier National Events

### MLS NEXT Fest
- **When:** Typically June/July
- **Where:** Rotating cities
- **Who:** [[MLS NEXT]] teams only
- **Scout level:** Highest — MLS academies, national team scouts, D1 college coaches
- **Platform:** [[GotSport]]
- **Notes:** The single most scouted youth event in the US

### ECNL National Events
- **ECNL Boys National Playoffs:** June
- **ECNL Girls National Playoffs:** June
- **ECNL National Selection Game:** Top individual players
- **Scout level:** Very high — D1 coaches, national team staff
- **Platform:** [[TGS AthleteOne]] (moving to [[GotSport]] June 2026)

### GA (Girls Academy) Showcase Events
- **GA National Showcase:** Spring
- **GA Cup:** Summer
- **Scout level:** Very high for girls
- **Notes:** ASPIRE tier events launched Feb 2025 — see [[Recent Changes 2024-2026]]

---

## Tier 2 — Major Regional/National

### Dallas Cup
- **When:** March/April
- **Where:** Dallas, TX
- **Teams:** 200+ from 15+ countries
- **Scout level:** High — international exposure
- **Platform:** [[GotSport]] (Event ID 45488 for 2026)
- **Notes:** One of the oldest and most prestigious youth tournaments globally

### Jefferson Cup
- **When:** March
- **Where:** Richmond, VA
- **Teams:** 700+
- **Scout level:** High — East Coast D1/D2/D3 coaches
- **Platform:** [[GotSport]]

### Surf Cup
- **When:** July/August
- **Where:** San Diego, CA
- **Teams:** 500+
- **Scout level:** High — West Coast focus
- **Platform:** [[GotSport]]

### Players Showcase
- **When:** Multiple dates (fall/spring)
- **Where:** Various locations
- **Scout level:** High — recruiting-focused format
- **Platform:** [[GotSport]]

### Weston Cup
- **When:** January
- **Where:** Weston, FL
- **Teams:** 300+
- **Scout level:** Moderate-High — Southeast/international
- **Platform:** [[GotSport]]

---

## Tier 3 — Strong Regional

### Disney Soccer Showcase
- **When:** November/December
- **Where:** Orlando, FL (ESPN Wide World of Sports)
- **Scout level:** Moderate

### Potomac Memorial Tournament
- **When:** May (Memorial Day weekend)
- **Where:** DC Metro area
- **Scout level:** Moderate

### State Cup Championships
- **When:** Varies by state (typically spring)
- **Where:** Each state
- **Scout level:** Moderate (state-level)
- **Notes:** Organized by [[Governing Bodies|USYS state associations]]
