# MLS NEXT

MLS NEXT is the **#1 boys league** in US youth soccer, operated by Major League Soccer. It serves as the primary development pathway to MLS professional academies.

---

## Structure

### Homegrown Division (Tier 1)
- **30 MLS academies** — direct professional pathway
- **122 Elite clubs** — top independent academies
- Highest level of youth competition in the country

### Academy Division (Tier 2)
- **220+ clubs** across 15 regional divisions
- Broader access point into the MLS NEXT ecosystem
- Pathway to promotion into Homegrown Division

### Age Groups
- U13, U14, U15, U16, U17, U18, U19
- All play 11v11

> [!info] U13 Format Change
> U13 games use a unique format: **three 25-minute periods** instead of the standard two halves. This affects game data parsing in the [[Data Pipeline]].

## Age Group Policy

> [!warning] Birth Year Cutoff Exception
> Unlike most leagues shifting to school-year age groups in 2026-27, **MLS NEXT stays on birth year cutoff**. This creates a split in the ecosystem — see [[Age Groups and Birth Years]] and [[Recent Changes 2024-2026]] for details.
