# Tiger Tournaments

Tiger Tournaments is a youth soccer tournament organization based in Texas. It's the first client organization using the [[Evo Draw Home|Evo Draw]] platform.

---

## Active Projects

- [[Evo Draw Home|Evo Draw]] — data platform powering rankings, predictions, and bracket generation

## Events

---

## What Tiger Tournaments Does

- Organizes and runs youth soccer tournaments
- Manages team registrations, bracket generation, and scheduling
- Provides rankings and game history for registered teams
- Uses [[Evo Draw Home|Evo Draw]] as the underlying technology platform

---

## Key Relationships

- **GotSport** — event hosting platform (event management, registration, scheduling)
- **Clubs & Coaches** — customers who register teams for events
- **Evo Draw** — internal platform brand (the technology, not the client-facing name)

> [!info] Branding Note
> **Tiger Tournaments** is the client-facing brand. **Evo Draw** is the platform/technology brand. They are separate — Evo Draw could serve other tournament organizers in the future.
