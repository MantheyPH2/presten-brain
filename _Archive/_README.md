# Archive

**Move stale projects and obsolete notes here. Never delete.**

If something is done, paused indefinitely, or no longer relevant — it lives here. The archive keeps your active folders clean without losing context.

## What goes here

- Completed projects (set `status: archived` in frontmatter)
- Obsolete research or ideas that didn't go anywhere
- Old meeting notes you don't need quick access to
- Anything from the inbox that sat for 3+ weeks untouched

## What stays out

- Reference material — that stays in `06 - Reference/` even if old
- Journal entries — those stay in `01 - Personal/Journal/` as a timeline
